# Kairos Timer — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview

**Kairos Timer** ("the App") is developed by Pedro Vieira ("Developer").

## Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All timer and phase configuration data is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never sent to any server.

## Internet Access

The App does not use internet access. No network requests are made.

## Permissions

The App does not request any Android permissions.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to this policy will be reflected in an updated version of this document.
